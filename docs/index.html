Explore a vast, ruined world

Awakened among a pile of rubble and unsure of what's going on, you're sent to explore the remains of an underground tunnel network. Talk to survivors, navigate the spiderweb of tunnels, and piece together what's left of the world.

Find your own path

Go wherever you want, whenever you want. Explore alternate routes, discover lone survivors, fight hostile wildlife, or simply sit back and take in the atmosphere.

Discover the secrets of the past

Not everything has been pillaged completely, and some ruins from the old world still lay dormant. Find your way around their security, use the destruction of the calamity to your advantage, and discover remnants of a society that met its end.
